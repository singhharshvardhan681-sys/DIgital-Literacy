# Responsible Social Media Use Checklist 

## Do's 
- Think before posting any content online.
- Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
- Verify information before sharing it.
- Respect others' opinions and communicate politely.
- Manage privacy settings regularly.

## Don'ts 
- Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.
- Do not spread fake news or unverified content.
- Avoid posting offensive or harmful comments.
- Do not use social media during important academic activities excessively.
- Avoid accepting requests from unknown people without checking profiles.
