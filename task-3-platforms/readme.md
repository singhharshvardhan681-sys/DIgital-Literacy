## Introduction  
This project was created to spread awareness about digital literacy through an online quiz. Digital literacy means the ability to use digital devices, internet tools, and online platforms safely and effectively. The quiz was designed to test basic knowledge of safe internet practices, password security, email awareness, and responsible digital behavior.


## Platform Used  
- Google Forms for creating the quiz  
- Google Sheets for collecting and analyzing responses  


## Steps to Create the Google Form  
1. Open Google Forms in browser  
2. Click **Blank Form**  
3. Give the title **Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz**  
4. Add description explaining purpose of quiz  
5. Create 5 questions  
6. Select **Multiple Choice** for 4 MCQs  
7. Select **Short Answer** for 1 descriptive question  
8. Turn on **Required** option for all questions  
9. Open **Settings** and enable **Make this a quiz**  
10. Assign correct answers and marks  
11. Share form link with participants  
12. View responses automatically in Google Sheets  


## Questions Included in the Quiz  

### MCQ Questions  
1. What does digital literacy mean?  
2. Which of the following is the safest password?  
3. What should you do if you receive a suspicious email?  
4. Which of the following is good digital behavior?  

### Short Answer Question  
5. Why is digital literacy important in daily life?
